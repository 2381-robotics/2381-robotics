About Us

2381 Robotics is a high school robotics club run at Colonel By Secondary School in Ottawa, Ontario. Since its establishment in 2015, the club has inspired years of innovation and Colonel By with multiple competitive teams working on robots for VEX EDR Robotics Competitions where competitors are given a yearly challenge for which they must design, build, program, and drive a robot to complete.

Tower Takeover

The 2019-2020 VEX Robotics Competition Tower Takeover is played on a 12’x12’ square field. Two alliances, one “red” and one “blue”, composed of two teams each, compete in matches consisting of a fifteen second autonomous period, followed by a one minute and forty-five second driver controlled period. The object of the game is to attain a higher score than the opposing alliance by placing cubes in towers, or scoring cubes in goals.

About Our Teams

2381 robotics currently boasts 6 competitive teams, 2381A, 2381C, 2381D, 2381W, 2381Y, and 2381Z which participate in VEX Robotics Competitions. We have a very diverse set of bright students coming from all over the city of Ottawa due to the prestigious International Baccalaureate (IB) program offered at Colonel By. Our hardworking members are dedicated individuals who frequently travel around Ontario in order to compete against other VEX teams. Since the creation of our club in 2015, our success has served as inspiration and paved a path for future teams as the club continues to grow.
